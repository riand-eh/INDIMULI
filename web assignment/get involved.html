Get Involved with Phintan Wildlife Sanctuary

At Phintan, we believe in the magic of togetherness. Protecting Africa's wildlife isn't just a mission; it's a movement—and it needs you. Whether you have time, talent, or treasure to offer, there's a place for you here.

Make a Donation

Every donation, no matter the size, becomes a stepping stone toward saving wildlife. From funding anti-poaching patrols to supporting habitat restoration efforts, your generosity makes real impact. And yes, the elephants might trumpet their thanks!

Volunteer Your Time

Want to get your hands dirty for a good cause? Join us as a volunteer. You could help with wildlife monitoring, organize local cleanup drives, or educate communities about conservation. Bonus: incredible sunsets and lifelong memories are part of the deal.

Adopt an Animal

Ever dreamed of saying, “That lion is kind of mine”? With our animal adoption program, you can! Symbolically adopt one of Africa's icons and receive exclusive updates about your animal and its habitat.

Become a Member

Join the Phintan family as a member, and you'll gain access to special events, behind-the-scenes tours, and an exclusive newsletter packed with wildlife news and heartwarming stories.

Attend Our Events

From wildlife safaris to conservation workshops, our events are as thrilling as they are educational. Come for the fun, stay for the inspiration—and maybe a selfie with a giraffe in the background.

Spread the Word

Knowledge is power! Be a voice for the voiceless by sharing our mission online, at work, or with friends. Together, we can amplify the call to save Africa's incredible wildlife.

Shop with Purpose

Snag some eco-friendly, wildlife-themed swag from our shop. It's not just a shopping spree—it’s a direct contribution to saving habitats and protecting endangered species.

Leave a Legacy

Ensure future generations inherit a thriving planet. Consider leaving a lasting impact through planned giving or sponsorship opportunities that directly support our conservation programs.
