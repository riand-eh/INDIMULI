Where the lions roar, the hyenas laugh, and the dik-diks... well, they just look cute. Let’s save the wild—one adorable pawprint at a time.

About Us

At Phintan Wildlife Sanctuary, we believe every creature deserves a shot at stardom—from the swaggering warthog to the oddly charismatic bat-eared fox. Our mission? To protect them, share their stories, and make sure they’re around long enough for future generations to ask, “Wait, what’s a dik-dik?”
